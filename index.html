About Beavers

Beavers are fascinating creatures known for their impressive engineering skills and ecological impact. They are semi-aquatic rodents with unique adaptations:

  • View Beaver Images
  • Beavers build dams and lodges using sticks and mud.
  • Their sharp teeth never stop growing, allowing them to cut down trees.

Fun Facts

  • Beavers are the second-largest rodents in the world after capybaras.
  • They can stay submerged underwater for up to 15 minutes.
  • Beaver dams provide habitat for other wildlife.
